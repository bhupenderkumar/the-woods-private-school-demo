Why Families Choose Us

The Woods Private School — The Difference

Discover why families across Houston trust us with their children's education.

👩‍🏫

Expert Faculty

Our teachers are passionate, experienced, and dedicated to helping your child succeed. We have a low teacher-to-student ratio, ensuring that every child receives the attention and support they need to thrive.

📚

Rigorous Academics

Our curriculum is designed to challenge and engage your child, with a focus on critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills. We offer a range of courses and electives to suit every interest and talent.

🏠

Safe and Supportive Campus

Our campus is a safe and welcoming space for your child to learn and grow. We have a zero-tolerance policy for bullying and harassment, and our faculty and staff are trained to provide emotional support and guidance when needed.

🌎

Whole-Child Education

We believe in educating the whole child, not just the mind. Our programs and activities are designed to foster social-emotional growth, physical fitness, and character development, ensuring that your child is well-rounded and prepared for success in all areas of life.

🤝

Strong Community

Our community is a vital part of the Woods experience. We offer a range of extracurricular programs, clubs, and activities that encourage socialization, teamwork, and leadership development. Our parents are also an integral part of our community, with many volunteering their time and talents to support our students and faculty.

🎓

College Preparation

We're committed to helping your child succeed in college and beyond. Our faculty and staff work closely with students to develop a college readiness plan, and we offer a range of resources and support to help them achieve their goals.
