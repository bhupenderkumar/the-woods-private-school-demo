Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program is designed for children who are 2-4 years old and focuses on play-based learning, socialization, and basic skills development.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves students from Kindergarten to 4th Grade and emphasizes reading, writing, math, and science skills, as well as social studies and character development.

📝

Middle/Upper

Our Middle/Upper program serves students from 5th to 8th Grade and focuses on advanced academic skills, critical thinking, and problem-solving, as well as character development and community service.
