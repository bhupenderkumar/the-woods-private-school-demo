Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-Kindergarten program is designed for children aged 3-5 years old, where we focus on social, emotional, and cognitive development through play-based activities and hands-on learning experiences.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves students from Kindergarten to 4th Grade, where we focus on building a strong foundation in reading, writing, mathematics, and science through project-based learning and hands-on activities.

🚀

Middle/Upper

Our Middle/Upper program serves students from 5th to 8th Grade, where we focus on developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and communication skills through STEM education, arts, and humanities.
