About The Woods Private School

The Woods Private School is a vibrant community of learners, located in the heart of Houston, TX. Our school is built on a foundation of academic rigor, creative expression, and character development, providing students with a well-rounded education that prepares them for success in an ever-changing world.

Our experienced and passionate educators are committed to creating a supportive and inclusive environment, where each student is valued and encouraged to thrive. We believe that every child has a unique voice and perspective, and we strive to provide opportunities for them to express themselves and make a positive impact in their community.

From our state-of-the-art facilities to our diverse range of extracurricular activities, we are dedicated to providing a world-class education that inspires a love of learning, promotes social responsibility, and fosters a sense of wonder and curiosity in all our students.
