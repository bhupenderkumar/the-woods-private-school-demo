Our Mission & History

Established in 2005, The Woods Private School has been a beacon of academic excellence and character development in Houston. Our school's rich history is built on a foundation of strong relationships, innovative teaching, and a commitment to helping each student grow into a capable and compassionate individual.

At The Woods Private School, we believe that every child is unique and deserves a tailored approach to learning. Our experienced faculty use a variety of teaching methods to engage students, foster creativity, and promote critical thinking. This approach enables our students to develop a deep understanding of the subject matter and apply it in meaningful ways.

Beyond the classroom, The Woods Private School offers a wide range of extracurricular activities and programs that help students develop important life skills, such as teamwork, communication, and leadership. Our students participate in sports, music, art, and community service projects, which help them build lasting relationships and a sense of purpose.
