Our Mission & History

The Woods Private School was founded in 1995 by a group of dedicated educators who shared a vision of providing a high-quality education to students in the heart of Houston. Over the years, our school has grown and evolved to meet the changing needs of our community, but our commitment to academic excellence and character development has remained steadfast.

At The Woods Private School, we believe that every child learns differently and at their own pace. Our teachers use a variety of teaching approaches to engage students and help them reach their full potential. From hands-on activities to project-based learning, we strive to make learning fun and relevant.

In addition to academic rigor, we also place a strong emphasis on extracurricular activities and character development. Our students have the opportunity to participate in sports teams, music programs, and community service projects that help them develop important life skills and values.
