About The Woods Private School

The Woods Private School is a private institution in Houston, TX, committed to providing a well-rounded education that combines academic rigor with extracurricular activities and community service.

Our campus is designed to promote collaboration, creativity, and critical thinking, with state-of-the-art facilities and cutting-edge technology.

We strive to create a supportive and inclusive community that values diversity, empathy, and mutual respect, preparing our students to become engaged and responsible global citizens.
