Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program prepares young learners for the transition to elementary school with a play-based curriculum that emphasizes socialization, creativity, and early literacy skills.

📚

Elementary

Our elementary program builds on the foundations established in Pre-K, offering a rich and engaging curriculum that includes language arts, math, science, and social studies, as well as music, art, and physical education.

📊

Middle/Upper

In our middle and upper school programs, students delve deeper into their subjects, developing critical thinking, research, and presentation skills. We also offer a range of electives and extracurricular activities to suit diverse interests.

💻

STEM

Our STEM program offers a rigorous and interdisciplinary curriculum that prepares students for success in science, technology, engineering, and math fields. We provide hands-on learning experiences, coding, and robotics, as well as collaborations with local tech companies.

🎭

Arts

Our arts program fosters creativity and self-expression, offering courses in music, theater, dance, and visual arts. Students develop their talents, build confidence, and learn to appreciate the value of artistic expression.

🏈

Athletics

Our athletics program emphasizes teamwork, sportsmanship, and physical fitness, offering a range of team sports and individual activities for all skill levels. We believe in the importance of competition, but also in the value of participation and fun.
