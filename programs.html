Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program is designed for children aged 3-5 and focuses on social-emotional development, literacy, and numeracy skills.

📚

Elementary

Our Elementary program serves students from Kindergarten to 4th grade and emphasizes hands-on learning, creativity, and critical thinking.

🤔

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper School programs serve students from 5th to 8th grade and focus on academic rigor, character development, and leadership skills.

🔬

STEM

Our STEM program offers courses in science, technology, engineering, and math, and is designed to inspire creativity, curiosity, and problem-solving skills.

🎭

Arts

Our Arts program includes courses in music, visual arts, drama, and dance, and is designed to foster creativity, self-expression, and appreciation for the arts.

🏀

Athletics

Our Athletics program offers a range of sports teams for students, including soccer, basketball, and volleyball, and is designed to promote physical fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship.
