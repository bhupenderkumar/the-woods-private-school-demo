Our Programs

Early Childhood Program

A play-based curriculum for preschool and kindergarten students, focusing on social, emotional, and cognitive development

Elementary School Program

A comprehensive curriculum for grades 1-5, emphasizing literacy, numeracy, science, and social studies, with specialist classes in art, music, and physical education

Middle School Program

A challenging and engaging curriculum for grades 6-8, preparing students for high school with a focus on critical thinking, problem-solving, and leadership skills

High School Program

A rigorous and college-preparatory curriculum for grades 9-12, offering advanced courses, electives, and extracurricular activities to support students' interests and passions

Summer Enrichment Program

A range of summer camps and courses for students of all ages, providing opportunities for academic enrichment, creative expression, and outdoor adventure
