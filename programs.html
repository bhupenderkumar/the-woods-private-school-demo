Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten/Early Childhood

Our Pre-K program provides a nurturing environment where students can explore, create, and learn through play. Our experienced teachers use a variety of techniques to engage students and lay the foundation for future academic success.

📚

Elementary School (K-4)

In our Elementary School, students build on the foundation established in Pre-K, developing their literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking skills. Our teachers use a variety of teaching methods to engage students and make learning fun and interactive.

📖

Middle School (5-8)

In our Middle School, students begin to explore more advanced subjects, such as science, social studies, and language arts. Our experienced teachers use a variety of teaching methods to engage students and help them develop a deeper understanding of the subject matter.

🔬

STEM Program

Our STEM program provides students with hands-on experience in science, technology, engineering, and math. Our experienced teachers use a variety of techniques to engage students and help them develop problem-solving and critical thinking skills.

🎨

Arts Program

Our Arts program provides students with a variety of creative outlets, including music, art, and drama. Our experienced teachers use a variety of techniques to engage students and help them develop their creativity and self-expression.

🏈

Athletics Program

Our Athletics program provides students with opportunities to participate in a variety of sports, including soccer, basketball, and volleyball. Our experienced coaches use a variety of techniques to engage students and help them develop teamwork and communication skills.
